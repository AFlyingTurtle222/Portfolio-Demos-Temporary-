Here are three semi-functional projects I've worked on.

Parry:
WASD to move, click to parry. Anything red does damage and can be parried. Parry enough times to regain health.

Bullet Hell:
WASD to move, click to shoot, space to dash. Dash gives invincibility during the dash.

Mark of Darkness:
Extremely buggy, only really meant to show off the room generation, movement, and enemy system.
WASD to move, right click to dash. (The attacks and cards are currently disfunctional, will be fixed at a later date)
